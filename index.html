About Us

Welcome to Garibaldi Mexican Restaurant, a culinary haven deeply rooted in family traditions. Established in 2000, our restaurant is a testament to the perseverance and passion of the original owners, who remain at the helm to this day. Hailing from Mexico, the family's journey involved hard work in various fields until their culinary calling beckoned. With a commitment to authenticity, we bring you genuine Mexican flavors that reflect our rich heritage. Over the years, our dedication to quality has led to the opening of multiple restaurants, each telling a unique story. Some ventures thrived, others found new homes with cherished clients, all contributing to the expansion of our culinary legacy. Join us on a flavorful journey where every dish carries the warmth and authenticity of our Mexican roots.
